Creamy Chicken & Mushroom Pasta

A simple yet elegant dish that's perfect for a quick weeknight dinner.

Approx. 25 minutes

Ingredients:

  • Pasta (any kind) - 200g
  • Chicken breast - 1 pc
  • Mushrooms - 150g
  • Heavy cream (20%) - 200ml
  • Onion - 1 pc
  • Garlic - 2 cloves
  • Vegetable oil, salt, pepper

Instructions:

  1. Cook pasta according to package directions.
  2. Chop chicken, mushrooms, and onion. Saut√© in a pan until golden brown.
  3. Add minced garlic, pour in cream, season with salt and pepper.
  4. Simmer the sauce for 5-7 minutes until it thickens.
  5. Combine the cooked pasta with the sauce and serve.
