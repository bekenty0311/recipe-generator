Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 19, 2025

Welcome to FridgeChef ("we", "our", "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information on our website fridgechef.app (the "Service").

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Our Service is designed to be simple and privacy-focused. We do not require you to create an account, and we do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, or location.

The ingredients you enter into our recipe generator are processed to provide you with a recipe and are not stored or linked to you in any way.

2. How We Use Information

The non-personal information (the list of ingredients) is sent to a third-party AI service (Google's Gemini API) solely for the purpose of generating a recipe. We do not log or store this information on our servers.

3. Cookies and Tracking

We do not use cookies for tracking or user identification. Our site is stateless, meaning we do not store any information about your session.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our site. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to our website or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
